# Junior Business Analyst

## Professional Profile
Motivated Junior Business Analyst with practical training in requirements engineering and business analysis. Skilled in translating business needs into clear, actionable requirements and technical documentation.

## Key Skills
* **Requirements:** Requirements Elicitation, User Stories (INVEST), Use Cases, and Change Management.
* **Technical:** SQL, API (Postman), Git, and SDLC (Agile/Scrum/Kanban).
* **Modeling:** Visualizing processes via UML, BPMN, and ER Diagrams.
* **Tools:** Jira, Confluence, GitHub, Google Docs, and Figma.
* **Strategy:** Stakeholder Mapping, MVP Definition, Product Roadmapping, and Backlog Management.
* **Design:** Prototyping and UX Fundamentals.
